Motor Insurance

Cashless Claim

  • 1 .

    Inform your Insurer

    In the event of damage, that is, damage due to an accident, first and foremost, as a policyholder, you will have to inform your insurance company, and submit the duly filled claim form and also inform the police, to enable them to depute a surveyor.

    Don't attempt to move the vehicle from the accident spot without the permission from the police or the insurance company

    2 .

    Damage Assessment

    The insurance company will send a surveyor to assess the damages. The surveyor will prepare a report and pass it on to the insurer and you will also receive a copy.

    3 .

    Send your Car for Repairs

    In order to avail the cashless claim service you need to get your car repaired only at the network garages of your insurer. One advantage of cashless claim is you do not have to pay for the repairs upfront to the garage for the covered damages, the insurance company will pay only for the repairs that are covered in your policy directly to the garage.
